*****************Chopper Survival Quest*********************

Developed by : 
MUSA TUR FARAZI & Md AS-AID
Our first project in BUET 1-1 !!


This is a survival game basically where you have to avoid obstacles at any cost to gain score.

CONTROLS :
- Press up-arrow,down-arrow,left-arrow and right-arrow move the chopper,you can alternatively use w,a,s,d to control.
- You can mute the game by pressing 'm' and unmute by pressing 'c'.
- You can select levels from level options.
- You can check the high score of different levels.


INSTRUCTION :

Do open it with all the necessary files included and also dont not change the window size!! You will get 3 lives to survive!! Enjoy !
